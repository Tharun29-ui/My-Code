Neymar Jr. is a Brazilian professional footballer known for his incredible dribbling skills, flair, and goal-scoring ability. He has played for top clubs like Santos FC, Barcelona, Paris Saint-Germain (PSG), and currently plays for Al Hilal in Saudi Arabia. He is also a key player for the Brazilian national team.
